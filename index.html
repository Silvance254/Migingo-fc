About Our Club

Founded in 1975, Migingo Football Club has been a cornerstone of our community, fostering talent and promoting sportsmanship for nearly five decades. Our motto "No Guts, No Glory" embodies the spirit and determination of every player who wears our colors.

Known as "The Leopard of the Sky," our club represents strength, agility, and the relentless pursuit of excellence both on and off the field.

We pride ourselves on developing not just skilled footballers, but well-rounded individuals who contribute positively to society.

Our Coaches

Robert Mwangi - Head Coach

With over 15 years of coaching experience, Coach Robert has been with Migingo FC since 2010. He holds a UEFA B License and has previously coached several top-tier clubs in the region.

Coaching Philosophy: "Discipline, teamwork, and relentless effort are the foundations of success."

Specialties: Tactical analysis, player development, team management

Michael Odhiambo - Assistant Coach

Former Migingo FC captain, Michael transitioned to coaching after his retirement in 2015. His deep understanding of the club's culture makes him an invaluable asset to our coaching staff.

Coaching Philosophy: "Technical excellence combined with mental strength creates champions."

Specialties: Technical skills, fitness training, mentorship
